Security Services

Clients Security Solutions LLC provides professional security coverage tailored to your site, event, and risk level. All services begin with a pre-consultation and estimate.

Temporary Construction Site Security

Visible, consistent protection for active job sites. We help reduce theft, vandalism, trespassing, and after-hours incidents with patrol-based and fixed-post coverage options.

  • Jobsite presence & deterrence
  • Perimeter checks & access monitoring
  • Incident documentation & reporting

Shopping Center Foot Patrol

Professional foot patrol for retail and shopping center environments—focused on safety, de-escalation, and a calm presence for tenants and customers.

  • Property walk-through patrols
  • Customer-focused communication
  • Support for tenant concerns

Private Events Security

Security staffing for private events with clear boundaries, guest management, and a professional standard. We coordinate with organizers to help keep events controlled and respectful.

  • Entry presence & crowd oversight
  • De-escalation & conflict prevention
  • Coordinated event security plan
